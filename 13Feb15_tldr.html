How to fix a bank in 100 days

One of the concepts that I found intruging in this article is the idea of banks (and easy extrapolated to other industries) building or buying a “Challenger Brand”. This would be a separate company, owned 49% by the parent, with the option to purchase. With some amount of autonomy, this challenger could try other processes and products, then the parent could adopt those that work.

The man squatting on millions of dollars worth of domain names

After registering a bunch of good domain names, what do you do? While most may sell them at a good price and opportunity, the man featured in this article is looking to partner with companies. You bring the idea/product/service, he brings the domain. Really neat model.
