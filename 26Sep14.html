I'm far from a super-redditor, but I do enjoy reading from a number of subreddits, and try to skim the first couple of (highly curated) pages daily. One of the subs that makes the cut is r/RedditDayOf/. Each day it features a new theme, and today's is Esperanto. This reminded me of a story:

I was in the second round interview for the internship that ultimately led to my full time job. Having majors in computer science and criminal justice, I was exposed to a variety of preparation for interviews.
Towards the beginning of the interview, I was asked "what languages do you know?".
"Just English", I replied, not missing a beat but missing the question in its entirety.
(awkward silence)
Then realizing my mistake, I laughed, they laughed, and I carried on, "mostly C++, with some C, Python, and PHP." Thankfully I got the internship, and later the job.
